Philippa Louise Giibwa

Rising Author

A rising star in the literary world, Philippa Louise Giibwa has always had a passion for creating plays and novels. Originally from Uganda, but born and raised in Lesotho, a country in Southern Africa. She is currently studying Software Engineering in Rwanda. At 19 years old, she publsihed her first novel, titled 'Back to Black', and hopes to write more in the future.

Giibwa describes herself as a visual artist, graphic designer, gender equality advocate, tech enthusiast, and aspiring software engineer. She is highly passionate about technology, music, literature, languages, and visual arts.

Giibwa has always envsioned releasing books and novels that could help alleviate these worldwide concerns, along with her mission and goal of reducing disparities in gender in education and raising reading standards.
