Back to Black

"Back to Black" is a gripping tale of one woman's journey through love, betrayal, and heartbreak as she strives to heal from the wounds of her past. It explores themes of resilience, vulnerability, and the complexities of trust in the face of emotional trauma. Will Charlotte find the strength to pick up the pieces of her shattered heart and discover true love, or is she forever condemned to a cycle of pain and betrayal?

Burn for Me

"Burn for Me" is a dramatic novel of a teenage girl who struggles to cope with the aftermath of the loss of her beloved best friend, her father. This novel focuses on the themes of trauma, hurt, and loneliness. Sophia then uses her creative tool to express how she feels and talks about the things that have deeply hurt her, which she also addresses her father's death and how no one ever really cared about it or talked about it enough.

Chatroom Charade

In the world of AI and technology and where everything is computer based, "Chatroom Charade" addresses the consequences and the issues of depending solely on these in a humorous and comical way.

My dear, I'm queer

In the form of a diary young Gabby, "GaeBae", reflects and journals her growth and epiphanies. "My dear, I'm queer" is one of those 'coming of age' books that every pre-teen and teen must have and read. Gabby explores her body as she goes through her adolescent years and also discovers her sexuality along the way which becomes another obstacle that she has to tackle.

Sweet Little Unforgettable Thing

"Sweet Little Unforgettable Thing" is a juicy and drama filled novel for the older readers. This novel touches on the feeling of being mis labelled and how society views women for having more than 1 partner. Will this young woman turn all these rumours around and create a good name for herself or will she let the stories create her?
